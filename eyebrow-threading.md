### Why Choose Threading for Your Eyebrows?

**Eyebrow threading** is an ancient Middle Eastern and South Asian hair removal technique that offers several advantages over other methods:

- **Precision**: Threading allows for extremely precise hair removal, perfect for creating clean, defined brow shapes
- **Gentle on skin**: No chemicals, wax, or heat means less irritation and perfect for sensitive skin
- **Long-lasting results**: Removes hair from the root for results lasting 3-6 weeks
- **Better for sensitive skin**: Ideal if you use retinol, acids, or have reactive skin
- **No ingrown hairs**: Threading removes hair in the direction of growth, reducing ingrown hairs
