### Brazilian Waxing Aftercare

To maintain your results and prevent irritation:

**First 24-48 Hours:**
- Avoid hot baths, saunas, or steam rooms
- No tight clothing - wear loose, breathable fabrics
- Skip the gym to avoid friction and sweat
- No swimming in chlorinated pools
- Avoid sun exposure or tanning

**Ongoing Care:**
- Exfoliate 2-3 times per week (starting 48 hours after waxing)
- Moisturize daily with fragrance-free lotion
- Avoid touching the area with dirty hands
- Book your next appointment in 4-6 weeks for best results
