### Lash Lift Aftercare

**First 24 Hours (Critical!):**
- Keep lashes completely dry
- No makeup, mascara, or eye products
- Don't touch, rub, or manipulate lashes
- No steam, saunas, or swimming
- Sleep on your back if possible

**Days 2-3:**
- You can wear makeup and wash your face normally
- Gently cleanse the eye area
- Avoid waterproof mascara

**Weeks 1-8:**
- Use oil-free eye makeup remover
- Brush lashes daily with a clean spoolie
- Apply lash serum nightly for extra nourishment
- Avoid sleeping on your face
- Book your next lift at week 6-8
