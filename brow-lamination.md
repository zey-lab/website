### What is Brow Lamination?

**Brow lamination** (also called brow lift or brow perm) is a revolutionary treatment that transforms your natural brows by:

- **Lifting** each hair from the root for maximum fullness
- **Setting** brows in a uniform, upward direction
- **Creating** a fuller, fluffier "feathered" brow look
- **Taming** unruly or downward-growing hairs
- **Smoothing** gaps for a more complete appearance

### Aftercare for Long-Lasting Results

**First 24 Hours (Critical):**
- Keep brows completely dry
- Don't touch, rub, or apply any products
- Avoid steam, saunas, swimming
- Sleep on your back if possible

**Days 2-3:**
- Avoid excessive water exposure
- No swimming or heavy sweating
- Gently brush brows daily with spoolie

**Ongoing Care (Weeks 1-8):**
- Brush brows daily into desired shape
- Apply nourishing brow oil nightly
- Avoid oil-based cleansers directly on brows
- Avoid exfoliating directly on brows
- Book your next appointment at week 6-8
