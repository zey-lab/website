### Brow Tinting Aftercare

**First 24 Hours:**
- Avoid getting brows wet
- No face washing directly on brows
- Avoid swimming, saunas, steam rooms
- No exfoliating products on brows

**To Make Tinting Last Longer:**
- Use oil-free cleansers around brows
- Avoid exfoliating scrubs directly on brows
- Apply SPF to prevent sun fading
- Avoid chlorine (use goggles when swimming)
- Pat dry, don't rub brows

**When to Re-tint:**
Book your next appointment when:
- Color starts fading (typically 3-4 weeks)
- New hair growth appears lighter at roots
- You notice brows losing definition
